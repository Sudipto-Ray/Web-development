Some Fact About Cricket

Cricket is one of the oldest sports across the world. It Is played between two teams of eleven playerseach who scores runs. This is done by hitting the the ball across the boundary, or by runnning between two sets of three small, wooden posts called wickets. The wickets are at each end of a rectangle of short grass called 'the pitch'.

Origin of cricket: Cricket was probably created during Saxon or Norman times by children living in the Weald, an area of dense woodlands and clearings in south-east England that lies across Kent and Sussex.

Indian Cricket World Cup History

The Indian cricket team are two times World Champions. In addition to winning the 1983 Cricket World Cup, they triumphed over Sri Lanka in the 2011 Cricket World Cup on home soil. They were also runners-up at the 2003 Cricket World Cup, and semifinalists four times(1987, 1996, 2015, 2019).
